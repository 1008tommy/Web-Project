The History of MamaExpress Delivery and How It Came To Be

MamaExpress Delivery is a innovative business that surfaced in the flourishing landscape of e-commerce. Founded in 2014, our company began on its mission to transform the way people shop for everyday items, bringing convenience and affordability to their fingertips.


Our journey began with a straightforward idea; to close the gap between your traditional mama shops, treasured neighborhood stores, and the convenience of internet shopping. With a vast team of dedicated and enthusisastic individuals who understand very well the value of community and client-centricity, we stepped forward to create a seamless platform that would bring together customers with their favorite mama shops.


In our earlier days, MamaExpress operated from a modest office space, fueled purely by the passion and determination of our small but unwavering team. We worked tirelessly to construct a user-friendly website that would allow customers to browse smoothly through our wide variety of products from their beloved mama shops all from the comfort of their home.


To gurantee quick and methodical delivery, we implemented a new approach. Instead of relying regular logistics methods, we stationed our employees in close proximity to mama shops. This strategic decision allowed us to supply lightning-fast services to customers living nearby, minimizing delivery costs and times.


Recognizing the significance of technology, we invested in cutting-edge systems to streamline operations and enhance the MamaExpress experience. This allowed us to properly managed inventory , trace orders, and quickly get back to client queries, nurturing a reputation for our trustability and our professionalism.


Today, MamaExpress Delivery stands as a well respected name in the e-commerce business, trusted by many who rely on our platform for their everyday needs. We will continue to prosper and increase our reach, with future plans of partnering with other mama shop businesses across various neighborhoods to produce a diverse and sizeable product selection.
