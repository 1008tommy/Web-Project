The History of Mama Shop Delivery and How It Came To Be

We started a small indie company called Mama Shop Delivery. We created a website for people online so that they can buy cheap stuff in the comfort of their homes without having to travel to a nearby mama shop. So we will have an employee that stays near the Mama shop. When they receive a customer order that lives nearby the Mama shop from our website, the employee goes to the Mama shop and buys the items requested and delivers them to the customers.
